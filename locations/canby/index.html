About the Canby Location

Beyond just the on water experience we want to make every part of boating here in the pacific northwest as easily and enjoyable as possible for you.

At our storage facility in Canby, customers will have four different concierge storage packages to choose from to make for a truly hassle-free experience. The only thing you, your family and friends have to worry about is having fun out on the water, we will take care of the rest.

100+ Outdoor Spaces | 10 Covered Spaces | 72 Indoor Spaces

When you are ready to put your boat in storage, give us a call and we’ll come pick it up. Pick up the phone whenever you want it delivered back and we’ll drop it off at your doorstep. While your boat is at our storage facility, the Active Water Sports technicians can do everything from detailing to boat winerization.

For more information on the storage packages please give us a call – (503) 305-3328
